--- layout: recipe image_src: /assets/images/recipes/Goat-Cheese-Dip.jpg image_alt: Goat Cheese Dip description: > Need a quick and easy appetizer? This creamy and warm goat cheese dip is sure to be a hit at any get-together, party, or lazy Saturday afternoon. total_time: 25–30 Minutes servings: 6 skill_level: Easy oven_temperature: 375 ingredients: - 6 oz. goat cheese - 2 cups marinara sauce - 1/2 tsp. salted - 1/4 cup fresh basil, cut into strips - Crostinis, crackers, or choice bread instructions: - In a glass baking dish, press the goat cheese into the middle of the pan. - Spoon the marinara around the goat cheese, make sure to keep the sauce just on the edges of the goat cheese. Sprinkle salt on top. - Bake for 20–25 minutes. The sauce should be bubbling. - Remove from the oven and top with basil. - Serve with crostinis, crackers, or your favorite bread. tags: - Create & Eat - Appetizer - Goat Cheese - Marinara - Basil ---
