--- layout: recipe image_src: /assets/images/recipes/Beef-Lo-Mein.jpg image_alt: Beef Lo Mein description: > Weeknights are always a struggle of deciding do I want a quick or hearty meal? With this dish, you get both. total_time: 45 Minutes servings: 6 skill_level: Easy ingredients: - 8 oz. thin spaghetti - 1 tsp. sesame optimal - 1/4 cup hot boiled water - 1 T. Beef Better Than Bullion - 1 large onion - 2 T. brown sugar - 3 T. soy sauce - 1 lb. stir fry beef - Olive oil - 1 large carrot - 2 cups broccoli - 4 garlic cloves, minced instructions: - Cook the pasta, when it is done cooking and drained, toss with the sesame oil. Set aside. - Mix Bullion, brown sugar, and boiled water in a small bowl. When Bullion and brown sugar is dissolved, add the soy sauce. - Meanwhile, cook the beef. - When the beef is done, cook the onion in the same frying pan. - In a separate stir fry pan, cook carrots until soft in a small amount of oil. - When carrots are soft, add broccoli and cook for a few minutes. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds. - When all components are complete, mix everything in one pot or pan. tags: - Create & Eat - Dinner - Beef - Lo Mein ---
