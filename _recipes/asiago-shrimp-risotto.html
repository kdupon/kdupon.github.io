Toss in the shrimp, and you have the ultimate comfort dinner. total_time: 1 Hour 30 Minutes servings: 4 skill_level: Moderate ingredients: - 4 T. butter - 1 small yellow onion - 2 cloves garlic, minced - 1 1/2 cup arborio rice - 1/4 cup dry white wine (pinot grigio is a great choice) - 5 cups chicken stock - Desired amount of cooked shrimp - 3/4 cup asiago cheese, grated - 1/3 cup parsley, chopped instructions: - In a saucepan, heat stock on low while cooking rice. - In a large stockpot, melt 2 tablespoons of butter. - Add onion and garlic, cook until translucent. - Add rice and toast for one minute. - Add white wine, when the wine is almost absorbed, but there is still a little bit of moisture remaining, add 1 cup of stock. - When stock is almost absorbed, but there is still a little bit of moisture remaining, add 1 cup of stock. Continue this process 1 cup at a time until the rice is soft and tender. - Add cooked shrimp to rice. - Stir in cheese and 2 tablespoons of butter. - Top with parsley. tags: - Create & Eat - Dinner - Risotto - Shrimp ---
