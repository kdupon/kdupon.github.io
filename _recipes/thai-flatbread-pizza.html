--- layout: recipe image_src: /assets/uploads/photos/recipes/Thai-Pizza.jpg image_alt: Thai Flatbread Pizza description: > Sweet and tangy Thai pizza makes a great quick weeknight meal or tasty afternoon lunch. total_time: 30 Minutes servings: 4 skill_level: Easy oven_temperature: 400 ingredients: - 2 Naan Flatbreads - 1 chicken breast, cooked and shredded - 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced - 1/2 small yellow onion, sliced - 2 carrots, shredded - 1 cup broccoli, chopped - 1 cup mozzarella cheese - 1/2 cup green onion, chopped - 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped - 1/4 cup peanuts, chopped (Recommend chili lime flavor) sauce_ingredients: - 1/2 cup coconut milk - 1 1/2 T. red curry paste - 1 1/2 T. creamy peanut butter - 1/2 tsp. fish sauce - 1/2 tsp. soy sauce - 1 1/2 tsp. honey - Pinch of salt instructions: - Cook the chicken and shred when complete. - Mix the sauce in a small bowl. - Place flatbreads on a baking sheet, spread sauce. - Top with chicken, pepper, yellow onion, carrots, broccoli, and 1/2 cup mozzarella. - Bake for 10–12 minutes. - Remove pizza and top with remaining mozzarella, green onions, cilantro, and peanuts. Bake for another 3–5 minutes. tags: - Create & Eat - Dinner - Lunch - Pizza - Thai ---
