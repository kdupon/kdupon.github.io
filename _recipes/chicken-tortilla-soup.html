Excepteur sint occaecat cupidatat non proident, sunt in culpa qui officia deserunt mollit anim id est laborum. total_time: 45 Minutes servings: 6-8 skill_level: Easy oven_temperature: 350 ingredients: - 2 15 oz cans of black beans - 4 T. vegetable oil - 1 medium onion, finely chopped - 1 large poblano pepper, finely chopped - 3 cloves garlic, minced - 1 chipotle chili in adobo sauce, finally chopped - 1 tsp. adobo sauce - ½ cup crumbled feta - ¼ cup chopped cilantro - ¾ tsp. cumin - 2 T. mayo - 1 egg - ¾ cup panko breadcrumbs - ½ tsp. salt - ½ tsp. Pepper - Pretzel buns, toasted or Wraps - Condiment recommendations - chipotle mayonnaise, pepper jack cheese lettuce, onions instructions: - On a baking sheet, with aluminium foil, spread black beans in a single layer. Roast for around 20 minute or until beans split and outer skins become crunchy. Remove the beans from the oven and allow to cool. - Meanwhile, heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a medium sized skillet. Add onion and poblano, cook until vegetables become softened, around 5 minutes. - Add garlic, cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly. - Add chipotle and sauce, cook and stir for 30 seconds. - Transfer skillet mixture to a large bowl. - Take slightly cooled beans and crumbled feta and place in a food processor, pulse until the beans are about ⅓ of the original size. - Place the bean mixture into the vegetable bowl. Add cilantro, cumin, mayo, egg, and breadcrumbs season with salt and pepper. Mix contents of the bowl with hands. - Make patty sizes the same width as the pretzel buns. - Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a cast iron skillet (or large non-stick pan). Add patties to pan and cook for 5 minutes on each side. tags: - Create & Eat - Dinner - Burger ---
