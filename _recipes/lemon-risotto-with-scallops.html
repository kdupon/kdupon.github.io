--- layout: recipe image_src: /assets/uploads/photos/recipes/Recipe_Scallop-Risotto.jpg image_alt: Lemon Risotto with Scallops description: > Have you figured out by now that we are risotto junkies? This creamy lemon and herb risotto is a perfect base risotto which can easily be accompanied by seafood, different cheeses, or any vegetables you would like to add. In this case, we used scallops, and it was a perfect combination. total_time: 1 Hour 30 Minutes servings: 4 skill_level: Moderate ingredients: - 2 T. Olive Oil - 3 T. butter - 1 small yellow onion - 1 1/2 cup arborio rice - 1/2 cup dry white wine (pinot grigio is a great choice) - 4 cups chicken stock - 8 ready to cook sea scallops - Salt and pepper - 1/3 cup parmesan cheese, grated - Zest and juice of 1 lemon - 1/4 cup parsley, chopped - 1/4 cup green onions, chopped instructions: - In a saucepan, heat stock on low while cooking rice. - In a large stockpot, melt 1 tablespoon of butter and 1 tablespoon of olive oil. - Add onion, cook until translucent. - Add rice and toast for one minute. - Add white wine, when the wine is almost absorbed, but there is still a little bit of moisture remaining, add 1 cup of stock. - When stock is almost absorbed, but there is still a little bit of moisture remaining, add 1 cup of stock. Continue this process 1 cup at a time until the rice is soft and tender. - Stir in cheese, lemon juice, zest, and 2 tablespoons of butter. - In a saucepan, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Add scallops to pan and cook for 3â€“5 minutes or until brown. - Flip scallops and cook for an additional minute. - Top the risotto with scallops, parsley, green onions, and any remaining lemon zest. tags: - Create & Eat - Dinner - Risotto - Lemon - Scallops ---
