--- layout: recipe image_src: /assets/images/recipes/Thai-Lettuce-Wraps.jpg image_alt: Thai Lettuce Wraps description: > You won't be able to get enough of this healthy meal option; these lettuce wraps with creamy peanut butter sauce have become a go-to recipe in our household. total_time: 30 Minutes servings: 3-4 skill_level: Moderate ingredients: - 1 lb. chicken, cut to bite sized pieces - 1 T. olive oil - 2 T. red curry paste - 1 T. ginger - 4 cloves garlic, minced - 1 red pepper, largely diced - 1 cup shredded broccoli slaw - 1/4 cup hoisin sauce - Salt and pepper - Basil leaves - 4 green onions, chopped - 1/2 head of lettuce sauce_ingredients: - 1 cup peanut butter - 1/3 cup honey - 1 cup Asian sesame with ginger & soy dressing instructions: - In a medium saucepan, heat olive oil and cook the chicken. - Mix in the curry paste, ginger, garlic, red pepper, slaw, hoisin, salt and pepper, and basil. - When well mixed, add green onions. - Place lettuce flat on a plate spread the sauce on the lettuce, and add the chicken mixture. tags: - Create & Eat - Dinner - Thai - Wraps ---
