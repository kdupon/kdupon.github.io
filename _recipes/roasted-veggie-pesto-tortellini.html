--- layout: recipe image_src: /assets/uploads/photos/recipes/Veggie-Tortellini.jpg image_alt: Roasted Veggie Pesto Tortellini description: > The comfort of pasta combined with the freshness of vegetables makes for a balanced, flavorful meal. total_time: 25 Minutes servings: 3-4 skill_level: Moderate oven_temperature: 425 ingredients: - 1 red pepper, diced - 2 zucchini, halfed and mooned - 2 yellow squash, halfed and mooned - 1/2 red onion, diced - 2 T. olive oil - Salt and pepper - Grape tomatoes, halved - 2 cloves garlic, minced - 4 cup spinach - Cheese tortillini - Pesto - Parmesan instructions: - On a baking sheet, with aluminium foil, place red pepper, zucchini, squash, and red onion. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Cook for 10 minutes. - Add tomatoes and garlic, toss. Cook for 10 minutes. - Add spinach, toss. Cook for 1 minute or until spinach is wilted. - Meanwhile, cook tortellini. - Combine vegetables, tortellini, and pesto. Top with Parmesan. tags: - Create & Eat - Dinner - Pasta - Vegetables ---
